What is Odyssey?

Odyssey offers you a chance to showcase your skills in a plethora of events ranging from acapella and art to music and fashion. Every event is the culmination of months of hard work and meticulous planning. We promise to challenge you with a fresh and unique take on otherwise typical competitions.

Do you have the spark needed to rise to the occasion, and walk away with the trophy?
