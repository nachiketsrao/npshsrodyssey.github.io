Elysian

“The aim of art is not to represent the outward appearance of things, but their inward significance” - Aristotle.

Welcome to Elysian! An event that puts to test all the required skills of an artist. The event is designed in a way for artists to showcase their unconventional and ingenious ways.

Rounds:

  • Round 1: is a sketching-focused round; the participants are not expected to excessively detail their sketches.
    • A video will be live streamed.
    • The participants would be required to sketch three moments from this performance.
  • Round 2:
    • Qualified participants The participants must finish the painting by incorporating their assigned element. Once again participants will be required to submit the process pictures along with the final piece.
    • Reference pictures will be uploaded by November 6th, on the email provided by the shortlisted school, and an update will be posted on the Odyssey website.
    • Entries must be sent by November 8, 12 am
    • Any art medium of the participants’ choice can be used.

Rules:

  • This event is open for students from grade 9-12.
  • It is an individual event, with a maximum of 2 participants from each school
  • The video will be live streamed on 4th November from 6pm to 6:10pm on this website.
  • The entries for round 1 must be sent on the official mail id (events@npshsr.com) - by November 5th, 12 AM
