Opus

‘Poetry is the clear expression of mixed feelings.’
Do you wield a pen, not a paintbrush? If yes, then come visit the Opus gallery, where we paint masterpieces with words.

Specifications:
Venue: Library
Timings:
     - Round 1: 10:30 am - 11:15 am
     - Result Announcement: 11:45 am - 11:55 am
     - Round 2: 11:55 am - 1:15 am
Round 1 is a prepared round. Participants will have to choose from the topics given and prepare a poem from the point of view of an unconventional narrator.
4 participants advance to Round 2.
Round 2 will be on the spot. Participants will hear an audio clip following which they will have to write a poem based on their interpretation of the clip, in a specific style of poetry (eg. limerick, sonnet )

General Rules:
Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
In the case of profanity/vulgar language, the participant will be immediately disqualified.
Use of any electronic devices during the event is strictly prohibited.
No marks will be allotted for oral presentation.
Themes for round 1 will be given to the participants 3 days prior to the event.

Judging Criteria:
Originality
Relevance to theme
Expression and illustration
