Odeum

It is often believed that at the heart of every great musical performance is a symphony of instruments. Welcome to Odeum â€“ an event that contradicts this sentiment.
All teams will be assigned a western solo artist and a band as soon as they register for the event. The participants must use compositions from these artists to create a novel and original melody. They may choose to use pieces from only the artist or the band or combine both.
To make the event challenging, participants will receive a surprise element around 12pm, 7th of November. They must find ingenious ways to incorporate this into their musical piece.
On the 13th of November, all participants should join the Acapella meeting on Teams where the shortlisted videos will be live streamed and evaluated by the judges.
The assigned artists and band will be sent to the contact mail id provided by the schools

Rules:

  • Students from grades 9-12 are eligible to participate in this event.
  • Every school may send only one team, consisting of 4-6 members.
  • The final video (including the surprise element) must be approximately 3-5 minutes long
  • The performances must include some form of bodily percussion, i.e., clapping, snapping, stomping etc.
  • This is an acapella event and no instruments are allowed.
  • All submissions must be made by 4 pm, 11th November 2020 to events@npshsr.com.
