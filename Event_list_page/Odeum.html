Aera

Aera 2018 brings to you a one of a kind fashion event, set in the year 2050 we challenge your most innovative minds to tackle this year’s theme “Pellentesque: Futuristic fashion”. We’d like to see which one of you is the Soothsayer a la mode, of a specific region, in a world with advancing technology and fashion.
NPS HSR proudly invites you to Aera 2018, a platform for you to put on your best louboutins forward.

Specifications:
Venue: Central Courtyard
Timings: 2: 00 to 3: 00 pm
Number of participants - 10 ( 8 models and 2 designers)
Time limit – 4-5 minutes per school
Ramp measurements :
*will be confirmed once school reopens*

Each team will be required to create or edit an existing outfit using any material or prop ( like jenga blocks,cards,newspaper, cardboard, cloth material etc.) given on the day of the competition. Required materials for editing the outfit (like glue, needle, thread etc.) should be bought by the team. Your designing abilities will be tested, this outfit must be presented as the first outfit on the ramp.

All the outfits has to be designed with respect to a region (i.e. a country or culture specific)

A narration may or may not be provided by the designers. The use of electronic gadgets and the internet is not permitted during the duration of the event..

Designs must be original. If not made from scratch, each outfit must have an element made from scratch in them. Points will be lost for just putting together an outfit.

Teams MUST remember to use appropriate clothing. Any type of obscenity or vulgarity in clothes or gestures will lead to disqualification of the entire team. The usage of props is permitted. Hazardous props like candles, matches, sharp objects etc. will not be allowed

An audio CD containing the track and region selected must be sent to NPS HSR on or before 2nd July 2018 via email.

The track must be properly labelled with the school’s name and should only contain the soundtrack for the show. The participants must carry an additional copy on the day of the event.

Judging Criteria:
Design : unique, related to the topic
Outfits: Originality, Style, Appropriateness
Hair, accessories and props
Stage presence : Confidence, walk
Utilization of ramp
Usage of prop.

Negative marking for vandalisation of the given room.
