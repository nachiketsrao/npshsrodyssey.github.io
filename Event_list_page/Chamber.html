Armonia

If you think you’ve got your Pitch Perfect, and if your playlist includes a lot of Pentatonix, this is the event for you. NPS HSR presents, Armonía, our A Cappella event!
There will be two exciting rounds to test your skills, be sure to send the crème de la crème of your school!

Specifications
Venue: Central Courtyard
Timings: 10:00 a.m. – 12:30 p.m.
Composition of team:
Minimum number of vocalists per team: 4
Maximum number of participants per team (including beat boxers etc.): 6

Rules
Specifications of both rounds will be given to the schools 2 weeks prior to the event.
Three schools qualify to the second and final round.
The use of any instruments is prohibited.
Use of any song is allowed, however the mashup/medley and improvisation must be unique and original.

Judging Criteria
Creativity & originality
Stage presence
Musical appeal of the composition
Aptness to the theme
