Aphrodite's Chamber

“In order to be irreplaceable, one must always be different “ - Coco Chanel

Presenting Aphrodite’s Chamber! An ideal event for those who consider their style to be an innate part of their identity. For this event all teams will receive a moodboard, drawing inspiration from which they must create trendy outfits. Mood boards shall be sent to all participants as soon as they register. Following this, the participants must create a short video modeling their outfits. The main goal here is to express individuality and come up with a distinctive and remarkable outfit!

Rules:

  • This event is open for students from grade 9-12.
  • Every school may send only one team, consisting of 4-6 members.
  • All elements of the moodboards must be represented in the outfits. Points will be cut if this is not adhered to.
  • All members of the team must be part of the video and model the outfits.
  • The videos must be submitted by 8 November, through the official email id (events@npshsr.com).
  • The files must be in mp4 format.
  • Although Aphrodite’s chamber is not a live event, all the videos sent in by the participants will be live streamed on the day of the fest.
