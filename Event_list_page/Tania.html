Loquitor

Strap on your armour tight, have your battle stance at the ready, and make sure your quiver is full of arguments as you descend into the Loquitur arena to fight your opponents tooth and nail. Two exhilarating rounds to test your mettle and display your strength, to emerge victorious.

General Rules :


• Any profanity or use of abusive language will result in immediate disqualification
• Use of any electronic devices during the event is strictly prohibited.
• Statements for round 1 will be given to the participants a week in advance. The final statement will be given to the participants 10 minutes prior to the beginning of the round.
• Statements will be given to participants advancing to round 2 after the announcement of the results.
• 20 minutes preparation time for round 2 will be given to the participants.

Judging Criteria:
• Fluency
• Content
• Spontaneity
• Relevance to Statement
• Judge’s criterion
• Persuasiveness

Venue: Basement directly below the Central Courtyard

Timings:
Round 1 : 10 am - 10:45-11:00
There will be a break of 10 minutes for the judges to deliberate, and the results of the first round will be out by 11:10 am (latest)
Finalists will be given their statement and 30 minutes to prepare
Final round will commence at 11:30 am (11:45 latest) and end at 11:40 (latest)
