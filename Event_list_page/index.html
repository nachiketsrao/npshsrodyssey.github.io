THE EVENT LIST

From acapella to cooking, and from coding to design, there's something for everyone at Odyssey 2018.

Aera

Poise, Elegance, Allure.
Set in the year 2050, Aera, a one of a kind fashion event, challenges your most innovative minds to tackle this year’s theme “Pellentesque: Futuristic fashion”. We’d like to see which one of you is the Soothsayer à la mode, of a specific region, in a world with advancing technology and fashion.

Armonia

Rhythm, Melody, Timbre.
If you think you’ve got your Pitch Perfect, and if your playlist includes a lot of Pentatonix, this is the event for you. NPS HSR presents, Armonía, our A Cappella event! There will be two exciting rounds to test your skills, be sure to send the crème de la crème of your school!

Battle of the Bands

NEEDS WORK
Battle of the Bands is a culmination of of the soul of Indian and jazz music and the music of the past. We challenge you to let the music in your mind flow freely to create an exquisite blend of eras and genres of music. Music is your ammunition. Assemble the strongest army and be prepared, for this is war. We want to see which one of you can handle the heat of the battle. Send your best team to NPS HSR’s Battle of the Bands and may the best band win!

Connaissance

Empirical, Accurate, Conclusive.
Science is all about asking questions and finding answers but have you ever asked yourself how good you are at science? For the first time in the history of Odyssey, we present to you, a science event, “Connaissance” where you can find the answer. Make sure you’re the smartest, the wittiest and the sharpest, as the search won’t be easy!

Cucinando

Delectable, Flavorful, Appetizing.
A recipe has no soul, you as a cook must bring soul to the recipe. Bring out the best of your culinary skills through our event at NPS HSR, Cucinando. Two interesting rounds to bring out your proficiency in cooking and contest with other schools.

Error 404

PLACEHOLDER
NPS HSR brings to you Error 404, our coding competition specially designed to tickle your brains and a platform for you to code to the best of your abilities. Each team is given a set of questions. The team that is able to come up with the most solutions in the allotted time frame wins. So get your keyboards ready, your developments environments set up, and let the coding commence!

In Step

Lithe, Grace, Power.
Dance is not just an art form. It is a form of expression, it is a powerful impulse. It can be a form of therapy; it can be an inborn talent. It transforms emotions and breathes life into ideas. It expresses the inexpressible and explains that which cannot be explained. NPS HSR invites you to take part in our dance event. Come, show us what dance means to you. Come, ready for a fight, for a fight it will be!

Invictus

Dynamic, Driven, Enterprising.
Acquire some of the most popular cryptocurrencies on the internet in a Monopoly-styled quiz, charge players network fees for landing on your cryptos and collect money. Use your money to acquire more cryptocurrencies, build mining rigs and farms and become a Crypto Giant.

Kalon

Eloquent, Lucid, Chic.
Do you have what it takes to be Ms. /Mr. Odyssey? Does your charisma have the power to influence change? Do your thoughts speak to people? If yes, then Kalon is the event for you. Kalon is a personality pageant that has three rounds. “Minute to Win It” (Talent Round), Extempore Speaking and Ramp Walk.

Loquitur

Fiery, Silver-Tongued, Critical.
Strap on your armour tight, have your battle stance at the ready, and make sure your quiver is full of arguments as you descend into the Loquitur arena to fight your opponents tooth and nail. Two exhilarating rounds to test your mettle and display your strength, to emerge victorious.

Meraki

Vivid, Balanced, Intricate.
Odyssey 2018 presents Meraki,a spectacular design cum art event that promises to trigger your creative juices! Each group will consist of 3 participants and compete in both rounds. Marks will be allotted cumulatively throughout the event There will be no elimination, i.e, all groups will compete throughout the event.

Opus

Lyrical, Animated, Narrative.
Poetry is the clear expression of mixed feelings. Do you wield a pen, not a paintbrush? If yes, then come visit the Opus gallery, where we paint masterpieces with words.

Stage Craft

PLACEHOLDER
NPS HSR brings to you Stagecraft- a theatre event that ties ones creativity to their ability to emote. With one round challenging teams to portray, twisted and understated emotions and phenomenon, and another impromptu round testing their deepest instincts as an actor and as a team, craft your own story and own the stage.

Stand Up

Uproarious, Rambunctious, Hearty.
If you think you can talk the legs off a donkey, and make an audience member pay for it, then this event’s for you. The Stand-Up event brings to you a riot of stand-up comics, improv sessions and one-liners. Come well prepared though, as the competition here’s no laughing matter!

Treasure Hunt

Guarded, Mysterious, Unexpected.
This event, we take it up a notch, the clues are based on physics, chemistry, programming and logical reasoning and comes with the assurance of being challenging and different. So, if you’ve got the skills of a Niffler, or the ability to find an Aegis, this event’s yours.
