THE EVENT LIST

From acapella to cooking, and from coding to design, there's something for everyone at Odyssey 2020.

Odeum

It is often believed that at the heart of every great musical performance is a symphony of instruments. If you have it in you to prove this sentiment otherwise, then welcome to the Acapella event Odeum!

Aphrodite's Chamber

“In order to be irreplaceable, one must always be different “ - Coco Channel
Presenting Aphrodite’s Chamber! An event ideal for those who consider their style to be an innate part of their identity.

Mythos

In the words of C.S Lewis, ‘good literature adds to reality, it does not merely describe it ‘.
Named after the Greek word for stories, Mythos is a one of a kind events that tests your knowledge, writing as well as performance skills.

Pheme

Journalism is the art of using words as a weapon. True journalism creates the first draft of history. Introducing Pheme, the journalism event for Odyssey 2020!

Effradeia

“Music isn’t just a combination of vocal and instrumental sounds; it is an expression of emotions “

Elysian

“The aim of art is not to represent the outward appearance of things, but their inward significance “- Aristotle
Welcome to Elysian! An event that puts to test all the qualities of the perfect artist.

Terpsichore

In Greek mythology, Terpsichore, one of the nine muses, is revered for her magnificent dancing. Embodying this spirit, the dance event of Odyssey 2020 is bound to be a challenging, yet memorable one for all those with a passion for dance.

Tania

American animator John Lasseter once said that “short films have a sense of raw creativity and unbridled joy, that is different from anything you see in a feature film “. Get ready to display your ingenuity by creating an engaging short film for the event Tainia!
