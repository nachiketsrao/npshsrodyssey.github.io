Effradeia

“Music isn’t just a combination of vocal and instrumental sounds; it is an expression of emotions “

Presenting Effradiea! An event which gives bands an opportunity to enthrall, through myriad and magical harmonies. All bands must film a western music performance. Effradiea gives the bands freedom to choose songs of any genre, perform covers, perform only instrumentals or even an original piece. The best 5 performances will qualify for round 2, where they will be evaluated by an external judge. These qualifying performances will also be live streamed on the day of the fest. Participants are encouraged to get creative and come up with a truly dulcet composition!

Rules:

  • This event is open for students from grade 9-12.
  • Every school may send only one team, consisting of 4-6 members.
  • The final piece submitted must be 3-4 minutes long.
  • The final video must be converted into a google drive link and sent to the official email id for submission (events@npshsr.com).
  • All submissions must be sent by 10 November 2020.
  • If an editor is required to create the final video, he/she need not be part of the band
  • Autotune while editing is not permitted.
