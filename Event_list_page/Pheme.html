In Step

DANCE IS FREEDOM. DANCE IS LIFE.
Dance is not just an art form. It is a form of expression, it is a powerful impulse. It can be a form of therapy; it can be an inborn talent. It transforms emotions and breathes life into ideas. It expresses the inexpressible and explains that which cannot be explained.
NPS HSR invites to take part in our dance event, IN STEP.
Come, show us what dance means to you. Come, ready for a fight, for a fight it will be!

Specifications:
Venue: Basement
Timings : 10:00 AM onwards
Number of participants: 6 per team

Rules:

Round 1:
Dance Theme : “tribal or classical or folk dance portraying the cultural aspects of a particular state of India”
For example, bhangra or gida from Punjab
Time limit : 3 + 1
Narration may be included but within the time limit
Teams have the freedom to choose a state of their choice
Props can be used, but they will NOT fetch the teams extra points
A labelled CD with only the track should be sent to NPS HSR on or before 4th July 2018.

Round 2- (4 teams will be selected after elimination and will proceed to the 2nd round)
Each team will randomly be given a sound track and theme.
Each team will get 2 hours to prepare a dance conveying the theme provided to them
Each team will get 1.5 to 2 minutes to portray their theme
Narration may be included but within the time limit
Participants need not get their own music for round 2
Teams must bring appropriate clothing to suite the 2nd round

Note: Teams MUST remember to use appropriate clothing. Any type of obscenity or vulgarity in clothes or gestures will lead to disqualification of the entire team.

Judging Criteria:
Team work and co-ordination (Round 2)
Creativity – Interpretation and Depiction of topic (Round 1 and Round 2)
Choreography and Execution (Round 1 and Round 2)
Costume (Round 1)
Negative marking for vandalisation of the given room.

Requirements:
A room for each team to choreograph in (preferably the bigger rooms)
A tape recorder for each team to play music
An efficient sound system in the basement
Appropriate lighting in the basement
Banner
Artwork and decorations
3 judges: 2 external and 1 internal
Prizes
Volunteers: 9-10
