Pheme

Journalism is the art of using words as a weapon. True journalism creates the first draft of history. Introducing Pheme, the journalism event for Odyssey 2020!

Rounds:

  • Round 1: All teams will be assigned a topic, based on which they must create a short documentary style news piece. The news pieces must be engaging and informative, but more importantly they must be objective and maintain journalistic integrity.
  • Round 2:
    • The teams shortlisted for round two will be given 4 topics , each of them will be linked to a specific style of journalism. As they are presenting, they will be asked to randomly switch between the topics.
    • Participants will be given 10 minutes to prepare.

Rules:

  • Students from grades 9-12 are eligible to participate in this event.
  • All schools can send only 1 team, consisting of 2 members.
  • Submissions for round one must be sent by 8 November on the official email id (events@npshsr.com).
  • On 4 November, a meeting will be conducted by the event heads to assign topics and explain all guidelines.
