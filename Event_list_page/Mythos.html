Mythos

In the words of C.S Lewis, ‘good literature adds to reality, it does not merely describe it’ i. The event, Mythos, named after the Greek word for stories, is a one of a kind event that tests your knowledge, writing as well as performance skills.

Rounds:

  • Round 1: Exciting and fast paced literary quiz which will be administered on the online platform Kahoot!
  • Round 2: The shortlisted participants will be given a prompt, based on which they must write a ‘Tiny Tale’ of approximately 30 words. Participants will be given a maximum of 10 minutes to write their stories. This round tests the participants ability to weave a story and pack a powerful message in as few words as possible.
  • Round 3: The qualified participants will be allotted a character and a situation. They must then write and perform a 2-minute long monologue, embodying their assigned character. 20 minutes of preparation time will be allowed for this round.
In all, this event provides participants with a wonderful opportunity to display a multitude of skills.

Rules:

  • This event is open for students from grade 9-12.
  • This is an individual event. Each school can send a maximum of 2 participants.
  • All rounds will be conducted live. No prior submissions are required.
  • Any plagiarism will lead to disqualification.
  • Rounds 1 and 2 are both elimination rounds.
