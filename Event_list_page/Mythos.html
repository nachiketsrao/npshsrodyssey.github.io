Battle of the Bands

Awaiting Info....

Round 1:
Teams must prepare a song of their choice
However they must incorporate either a jazz instrument or an Indian classical instrument in their performance
The jazz or Indian classical instrument should shine in their performance
Results will be announced by 12:10

Round 2:
Option 1:
3 teams will be shortlisted
The contestants will be given three eras(80s’90s and 2000s) . They will have to choose a song from any one of these eras.
They will also be given their genres (pop, rock)
They will have to incorporate elements of the genre they choose into the song they picked from on of the eras given to them.
Option 2:
5 teams will be shortlisted to participate in round 2
Round 2 is the composition round
Each team will have to compose their own song which should not be more than 6 minutes
However there is a twist. Each team will have to pick up chits which have the scale and key signature they’ll have to transpose their song to
They will be given 30 minutes to transpose their song

Venue: Mini auditorium
Time:
Round 1: 10:00am-11:15am
15 minutes buffer time
30 min break
12:10pm- results of round 1
Round 2:
     Option 1:
     Total time -45 minutes to 1 hour
     Option 2:
     total time-1 hour 20 min
30 minutes to transpose the song(12:15pm- 12:45pm)
12:45-pm1:35pm to play the song
Other requirements: Mikes, collar mikes, amplifier, drum kit

Judging criteria:
Ability to bring out the jazz or Indian classical instrument (Round 1)
Transposing skills (Round 2 option 2)

Originality of song (Round 2 option 2)
Stage presence
Quality of performance
Number of judges: 3

General rules:
Each team must have 6-7 members
Each song must be 5-6 minutes long
The scale and key the contestants will have to transpose their song to will
not be revealed at around 12:15pm
Obscenity of any form is frowned upon
The judge’s decision is final
