Meraki

Odyssey 2018 presents Meraki,a spectacular design cum art event that promises to trigger your creative juices!
Each group will consist of 3 participants and compete in both rounds.
Marks will be allotted cumulatively throughout the event
There will be no elimination, i.e, all groups will compete throughout the event

ROUND 1- 10:30-12:00

GUIDELINES
1. Canvas (dimensions 2*3 ft.), paint brushes, paints, and other art materials are to be brought by the participants.
2. Participants may also bring jute, newspaper, plastic, sponge or any material of their choice for painting the canvas (optional)
3. The topic will be given on the spot.
4. Any medium of paint may be used.
5. Time limit- 1.5 hours

lunch break:12:00-1:00

ROUND 2 (MODELING) -12:00-1:00

GUIDELINES
1.3 materials will be given along with an abstract topic that is to be interpreted on the spot. -
2. The participants are required to create a model from these materials based on their understanding of the topic within an hour.
3. Paints, blades, knives or any such substances may be used for detailing (to be brought by participants)
4. Time limit- 1 hour
